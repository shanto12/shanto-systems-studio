# Demo Script

## 90 Seconds

Open the hero and point out the paired day/night motion states. Click Start Walkthrough, then move through Observe, Plan, Act, and Verify. Open the Systems map, filter by Security and AI, then show the Evidence timeline and Motion Lab. End in Demo Guide with the health status and release checklist.

## 5 Minutes

Show the hero transition and explain how it follows the tutorial pattern: paired visual states, local motion media, and section-by-section refinement. Walk through Workflow, Systems, Evidence, Motion Lab, and Demo Guide. Mention that the app is deployed on Netlify with CSP, health checks, mobile checks, and an evidence matrix.
